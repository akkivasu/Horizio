What does Horizio™ solve?

One of the biggest issues in running and working for a big firm is the intricate and highly levelised employee hierarchy that comes with it. While this hierarchy helps streamline business and technical processess, it also limits them and blinds them from diversity. Have a nice idea that could drive your company forward, but your boss is a personification of an ache in a rather unsavoury spot? Well, bad luck. Want to give feedback about practices and processes but HR isn't the easiest or best place to go? Bad luck again. Have a question you want to poll your employees/collegues on but can't go knocking on every cabin? Thrice bad luck. Fret not, Horizio™ is here to flatten that vertical hierarchy that's key to every firm and give it that killer edge that every startup has.
